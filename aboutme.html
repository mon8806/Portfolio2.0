Hello, My name is Monica Parente. I was born and raised in Seattle. Graduated from UW with my BA then enrolled in pastry school at South Seattle and earned my AA in pastry. I interned in France for 3 months after I graduated. Been baking for the past 8 years. Now, I'm currently a student at the UW Continuing Education Coding Bootcamp. Hopefully employer ready by the end of the school. The reason why I'm transitioning is that the thing I loved about baking is the creativity of it but it's not as challenging as it was in the beginning. I have a lot of different of hobbies and it all revolves my creativity. My husband suggested I try coding. I started learning on my own and I found that it wasn't as hard as I thought it would be. Just a lot of memorization. It still is challenging but it's what I'm looking for that also incorpates my creativity. I'm excited for this new journey. I know it's going to be tough but I won't give up.

On my free time, I really just like enjoying spending my time with my husband and our 2 little monsters, known as Nala and Kaizer aka our dogs. I also enjoy doing things around the house, gardening, woodworking and sewing. When the weather is on my side, I like to go hiking, camping and snowboading. We travel as much as we can. We've been to the Philipines, Cambodia, Korea and hopefully Italy and Spain for our honeymoon.
